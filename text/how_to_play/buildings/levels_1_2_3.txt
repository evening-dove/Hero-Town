The most basic building types. | Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 are all different categories, each of which contains 6 Buildings. | One Building per category will increase Production, Gold Per Turn, Defence, Food, Favor, or Happiness, by a set amount. | Level 1 buildings are unlocked at the start of the game and are the easiest the build, but often give the smallest rewards. | Level 2 and 3 Buildings tend to give bigger bonuses, but all need to be unlocked first, and require more time to pass before they can be completed. | | Level 1, 2, and 3 Buildings can only be built once per player.
